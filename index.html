About Our Office

Committed to Data Protection Excellence

The Data Privacy Office (DPO) of Central Luzon State University is dedicated to ensuring the protection of personal data and compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

Our office serves as the central hub for all data privacy concerns, providing guidance, training, and support to the university community in matters related to personal data processing and protection.

We work closely with all academic and administrative units to establish and maintain robust data protection practices that safeguard the privacy rights of our students, faculty, staff, and stakeholders.

Our Services

Data Protection Compliance

Ensure your department or unit complies with the Data Privacy Act through comprehensive audits, assessments, and guidance.

Privacy Training

Conduct regular training sessions and workshops on data privacy best practices for faculty, staff, and students.

Privacy Impact Assessment

Evaluate the privacy implications of new systems, processes, or initiatives before implementation.

Privacy Consultation

Provide expert advice on data privacy matters, policy development, and incident response procedures.

Incident Response

Assist in managing and responding to data privacy incidents and breaches in accordance with legal requirements.

Policy Development

Help develop and review data privacy policies, procedures, and guidelines tailored to university operations.
